Overview

At Dive Goblin, we take your privacy seriously (even more seriously than we take our love for underwater treasure). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

We promise to protect your data like a goblin guards their gold - with utmost dedication and perhaps a touch of obsession.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment providers)
  • Account preferences and diving certifications

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Website usage and interaction data
  • Shopping preferences and history

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Provide customer support and communicate with you
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send you relevant marketing communications (if you've opted in)
  • Maintain the security of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Unlike the cookies our goblins enjoy during break time, these are digital and help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Keep track of your shopping cart
  • Analyze site traffic and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Download your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
